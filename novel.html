Summary of Born A Crime

Noah explains apartheid, a system that divided South African society into racial categories, and
highlights the different approaches of Zulu and Xhosa tribes to resisting colonial rule.
This chapter introduces the theme of the minibus system, a symbol of economic and social
disparities that persisted after apartheid. Noah's personal story underscores the dangers faced
by Black individuals in South Africa during that period.

Chapter 1

The issues emerging from these chapters, such as the legacy of apartheid, the economic
